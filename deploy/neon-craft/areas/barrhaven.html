The Barrhaven market.

Barrhaven is one of Ottawa’s fastest-growing suburban communities, and it shows no signs of slowing down. With new housing developments in Half Moon Bay, Chapman Mills, and Longfields expanding the population year after year, the demand for local services — restaurants, trades, retail, healthcare, fitness, and professional services — is surging. For Barrhaven business owners, this growth is both an enormous opportunity and a competitive challenge.
